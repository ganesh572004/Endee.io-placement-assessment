# Product and Support Knowledge Base

## Refund Policy
Customers can request a full refund within 14 days of purchase.
Refund requests are processed in 3-5 business days.

## Password Reset
Users should click “Forgot Password” on the login page.
A reset email is delivered within 5 minutes.

## Enterprise Plan
Enterprise plans include SSO, dedicated support, and custom SLAs.
Pricing is contract-based and starts at 100 seats.
